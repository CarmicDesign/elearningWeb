About Me.

Meet Karma, a talented and passionate graphic designer with a keen eye for detail and a creative flair that makes his work stand out. Karma has been working in the field of design for over 10 years, building a solid reputation as a skilled and reliable designer with a talent for creating engaging and effective visual content.

Karma's journey in design began when he decided to follow his passion and undertake a Diploma in Graphic Design in 2020. However, this was not the start of his design career, as he had already been working as a freelance graphic designer since 2013, specialising in motion graphic design. He has worked on numerous projects with various clients, building up his skills and experience in the field.

Over the years, Karma has expanded his skillset to include digital and print designs, infographics, WCAG standards, inclusive design, and user-centered approaches. He is a skilled eLearning designer/developer with extensive technical skills ranging from Adobe Creative Suites, screencasting, animations, video editing, and Articulate Storyline with in-depth knowledge of triggers, variables, logic and Javascript, which enables him to develop next-level content.

Karma's work showcases his creative flair for digital design, motion graphics, and eLearning contents. He approaches his work with a pragmatic and analytical mindset, creating designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional. He is passionate about designing and producing projects that bring stories to life, creating engaging and immersive experiences for his audience.

In addition to his design work, Karma has worked as a Budget Officer for the Ministry of Finance in Bhutan for 8 years. This background has given him strong skills in interpreting and organising numerical and statistical data, and presenting complex information to non-technical clients through simple, understandable, and engaging visuals.
